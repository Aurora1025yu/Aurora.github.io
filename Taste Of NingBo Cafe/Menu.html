Menu Overview

We focus on providing world-class coffee experiences for coffee enthusiasts worldwide. We select high-quality coffee beans from around the world to bring you classic and unique coffee drinks, such as Italian espresso, latte, and cappuccino. At the same time, it is paired with our carefully developed exquisite snack series, such as croissants and cream cakes. As the seasons change, we also offer exclusive drinks and snacks to bring you a surprising taste buds journey.





Oat Latte

The perfect combination of full-bodied coffee and oats, with a rich and mellow aroma and a refreshing grain aroma, is both healthy and delicious.

 
Ice American style

Selected espresso blends with ice to create a refreshing and refreshing taste with a rich aroma. Each sip brings a refreshing and refreshing experience.

 
Cinnamon latte

The fragrant latte blends with the warm aroma of cinnamon, with a rich taste and every bite exudes an enticing sweetness and warmth.       

 
Butter dirty

This coffee perfectly combines butter sauce with espresso, with a rich and mellow taste, and each sip brings a unique taste enjoyment.

 
Almond caramel latte

A blend of rich espresso, smooth steamed milk, and unique almond and caramel flavors, with a faint almond aroma.

 
Concentrated latte

A rich concentrated coffee extracted from carefully selected coffee beans, perfectly blended with delicate and smooth milk.

 
Blueberry muffin

This muffin is decorated with fresh blueberries and paired with a soft cake body. With just one bite, you can feel the sourness and sweetness of blueberries and the richness of the muffin.

 
Rye bagels

This bagel is made from rye flour and undergoes a unique fermentation and baking process. The outer skin is crispy, while the inside is soft and chewy, emitting a unique wheat aroma.

 
Strawberry mousse

This mousse is based on fresh strawberries, mixed with chocolate and delicate cream and sugar powder. After careful stirring and refrigeration, it presents a smooth and delicate taste.
